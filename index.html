About Me

My name is Edith, and I'm from Veracruz, Mexico. I always wanted children, but unfortunately, it wasn't in the cards for me. So instead, I got dogs. At first, it was just one or two, but soon I became enamored with them and ended up with a small pack of my own.

I moved to Canada to start a new life and pursue my passion for dogs. I left my beloved pets in the care of trusted friends and family members in Mexico.

Since I arrived in Canada, I've been dedicating myself to learning all I can about dogs. I take care of other people's dogs, and it brings me great joy. I have become quite an expert in their behavior and needs, and I even took courses to learn more about canine behavior.

My ultimate goal is to open my own dog daycare center, where I can offer a loving and safe environment for dogs while their owners are at work. I know how hard it is to balance work and pet care, and I want to help make it easier for dog owners.

I have a natural gift for calming anxious dogs, and I always put their well-being first. I truly believe that dogs are wonderful creatures, and they deserve to be treated with love and respect.

If you're looking for someone to take care of your furry friend, I would be honored to provide them with the same love and care that I give to my own dogs. I understand how much dogs mean to their owners, and I promise to treat them like family.
